Dataset Summary

This dashboard is built on the Transformed_Housing_Data2.csv dataset, containing 21,609 residential property records. The analysis covers price segmentation from $250K to $600K+, structural attributes including bathroom, bedroom, and floor counts, and temporal factors such as house age and renovation history. The storyboard narrates four key findings derived from the data to help stakeholders make informed real estate decisions.
